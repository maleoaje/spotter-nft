How to mint your Spotter NFTS?

1

Download PHANTOM as an extension to your PC browser/Download the PHANTOM app (iPhone users only) and open a PHANTOM wallet.

2

Load your PHANTOM wallet wth enough SOLANA from your crypto wallet(Binance or FTX).

3

Go to https://spotternft.com & connect your PHANTOM wallet to the website. (If you are using a mobile device use the PHANTOM app BROWSER).

4

You may now mint your SpotterNFT- ensure that you have 1 Solana for public mint, 0.5 Solana if you are on the whitelist and few cents for gas fees.

5

After you have done the minting, the NFT is transferre to your wallet.

Any further questions email us at: hello@spotternft.com.
